# Worker safety (Niilox)

Tenant-scoped safety tooling for gig and field-work apps on the Niilox platform.

## Overview

Niilox provides worker safety capabilities for products where people meet in the real world. Apps opt in per `X-App-ID` and configure safety through the SDK or authenticated API.

Public documentation intentionally omits implementation detail. Registered developers receive the full integration guide, endpoint reference, and operational setup.

**Contact:** dev@niilox.com
